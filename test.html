About us...

Braiswick Photographic was established in 1965 operating from Colchester, Essex. Virtually all of our work is within the school environment, ranging from pre-school and toddler groups through to sixth form colleges.

School Photography

We have our own digital processing laboratory on site which caters for all of our production needs, in particular any special packages such as sepia, black & white or pictures with digitally produced frames and borders.

ID Services

We provide Proof of Age and Security ID Cards for school and business use. Proof of Age Cards are provided in conjunction with various Trading Standards Departments for 16+ students.
